Empowering People, Growing Together

The JOMPO community is the heartbeat of our mission. Our work is people-centered and built around empowering farmers, youth, women, and local leaders across rural and urban areas. We believe that strong communities are the foundation of sustainable development, and our programs reflect this commitment.

We organize forums and workshops where members can exchange knowledge, tackle local challenges, and co-create solutions. Our mentorship programs guide youth and women in entrepreneurship, environmental stewardship, and leadership development. Whether it’s through sustainable agriculture, renewable energy adoption, or digital innovation, we foster collaboration that leads to impact.

Our inclusive model prioritizes grassroots voices. We partner with schools to promote climate education, host sports tournaments to inspire youth, and lead cleanup drives that bring the community together. We don’t just build programs—we build relationships, trust, and local capacity.

Through every activity, from hosting climate action summits to tree planting and promoting clean energy, we aim to uplift lives and create a ripple of positive change. We welcome volunteers, partners, and dreamers who believe in the power of people and community.

Join the JOMPO movement—where local impact meets global vision. Together, we grow stronger.
