Knowledge and Tools

JOMPO offers a wide range of practical resources designed to empower individuals, groups, and communities working toward a greener and more resilient future. Our library includes downloadable manuals, training guides, video tutorials, and research reports tailored to real-world needs.

Whether you're a farmer exploring climate-smart practices, a youth group launching a conservation project, or an educator teaching sustainable development, we’ve created materials just for you. From how to set up a tree nursery to implementing drip irrigation or solar drying systems, our guides are field-tested and easy to follow.

Our content covers climate change adaptation, soil health, water harvesting, entrepreneurship, and renewable energy adoption. We also provide case studies, policy briefs, and success stories to inspire action and innovation across the region.

Stay connected and check back often—new resources are added regularly to keep you updated on the latest trends and techniques. We believe knowledge should be open and accessible, because informed action leads to sustainable change.
